The Youth Empowerment Society is a team of inspired young people who want to play their part in the development of the country as it rallies towards Vision 2016 and beyond. The team is referred to as Youth Empowerment Society, YES, and has been registered with the Botswana Department of Registrar of Societies. The society always intends to launch programs which provide a platform for youth to positively impact the community. The society therefore, beseeches interested youths and cooperates to share its vision and contribute to its fulfillment. All contributions and assistance to YES and its programs are held in very high regard
